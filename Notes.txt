Mouse:

Microcontroller		AVR Atmega168PA
Wireless IC		Nordic NRF24L01+

Programming pads (top row towards buttons, bottom row towards batteries
(same pinout for installed programming header


SCK	MISO	MOSI	SS
VCC	GND	Reset	VBAT

VCC (~2.2V) is switched after the DC-DC boost converter
VBAT	~1.5V (AA battery) 2x in parallel

SCK	Yellow
MISO 	Pink
MOSI	Purple
SS	Orange
Reset	Red
Ground	Brown
VCC	N/C
VBAT	N/C


Receiver:

Receiver is matched to the mouse (tested with another receiver and another mouse and neither worked)
